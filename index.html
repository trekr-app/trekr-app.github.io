Help is on

Enable Emergency Mode to send a text message with your current location to an emergency contact.

Trekr is a geolocation-based Android application designed to assist users in the navigation of trails as well as allow users to edit and maintain a local database of trails and points of interest. The motivation for this project stemmed from a mutual interest in outdoor recreation.
